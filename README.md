## Project Brief
Doctor Appointment App
This app helps you book doctor appointments easily. You can find nearby doctors, check their timings, and book a visit in just a few clicks. You can also get reminders for upcoming appointments and consult online if needed. It saves time and makes healthcare simple and fast.
